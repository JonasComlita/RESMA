# Monetization Guardrails

This document defines the strict product boundaries for RESMA's monetization framework. As an independent recommendation intelligence platform, user trust and data credibility are our primary assets. 

**Core Principle:** The observatory feed is a truth surface. Sponsored content must never be ranked alongside organic intelligence or masquerade as measured data.

## 1. The Core Feed is a Truth Surface
- **No Inline Advertising:** Do not blend paid content into the measured observatory feed. The "What's Surfacing Now" list must remain purely organic and data-driven based on the cohort, region, and platform selections.
- **Exclusion from Metrics:** Sponsored interactions are explicitly excluded from recommendation calculations, audience forecasts, and engagement analytics.
- **Ranking Independence:** Commercial partnerships cannot influence organic ranking or visibility in the central feeds.

## 2. Sponsored Modules Must Be Adjacent and Labeled
- **Right Rail Placement:** Monetization modules (e.g., "Sponsored Insight", "Brands watching this cohort") should be placed in designated "Adjacent Commercial Layers" such as the right rail or distinct header banners.
- **Visual Distinction:** Commercial modules must look different from core feed items. They should utilize distinct background colors, explicit border styles, or standalone container framing.
- **Explicit Labeling:** All commercial features must carry an explicit "Sponsored" or "Commercial" badge to avoid confusing users. The badge must be highly legible and clearly positioned.

## 3. Transparency for the Contributor
- The platform should make it obvious to contributors what is:
  - **Measured observatory data:** The core organic feeds and category rails.
  - **Aggregate intelligence derived from it:** Non-sponsored insights and reports based on cohort trends.
  - **Sponsored/commercial content:** Insights or campaigns paid for by third parties.
- A trust indicator or footnote should accompany sponsored items clarifying that they are "Excluded from observatory metrics."

By enforcing these guardrails, RESMA maintains its credibility as a research-first observatory while supporting sustainable monetization through premium intelligence and clearly defined agency partnerships.
